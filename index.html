Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Our school Church provides a place for the children to pay their respects and acknowledge those who bless us all with happy lives. Every morning the students go to the Church and we do a praying ceremony.



School Library

Was built in 1980

Our school Library provides children with the exciting opportunity to read books and learn new things. We provide this space in order to help students learn more and expand their knowledge so they can grow up to be intelligent, well-mannered and inspiring people.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

Many exciting and fun events have taken place in our school Auditorium, some of which include speeches, award assesmblies, theater plays from our CAPA groups and movie & trivia nights.

Inter School Science Project Competition
We won second place for Inter School Science Project Competition

We are very proud of our students for winning second place in this competition. Students were given time to work on their projects at home and at school. This year there was no specific theme for the projects and we had lots of wonderful submissions. Everyone was gifted with a participation medal to show how we appreciate the students efforts at our school. We encourage students to use their creativity and create some truly spectacular.

What's New?

  • New School Canteen

    Considering the food options and responsibilities, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have been in the past. Online menus with photographs of the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account on the school website that keeps track of their history of meal choices and any dietary requirements they may have. Ordering online by ticking a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss fo handling money making it safer and less complicated for staff and students alike. Here again, diversity is the key.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Accuracy - Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automated attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimise the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data.
    2. Economics - Have you ever thought that attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to innacurate time reporting
